# d/acc (Defensive Acceleration) – A Concise Introduction

## What Is d/acc?

**d/acc** stands for **Defensive Accelerationism**. It is a technological philosophy coined by Ethereum co‑founder **Vitalik Buterin** in November 2023. The core idea is to **accelerate technological progress, but with a deliberate focus on technologies that enhance defence, decentralisation, and democratic participation** – rather than on offensive or centralised capabilities.

d/acc positions itself as a middle path between **e/acc (Effective Accelerationism)**, which advocates unrestricted technological growth, and overly cautious regulation that might stifle beneficial innovation.

---

## Origins

- **Date of proposal**: 27 November 2023  
- **Venue**: Vitalik Buterin’s personal blog post titled *“My Techno-Optimism”*  
- **Motivation**: The post reacted to the rapid advances in artificial intelligence (AI) and the potential risks of superintelligent AI systems. Vitalik argued that we need a proactive, not reactive, approach to steering technology toward safety and public good.  
- **Later milestones**:
  - **August 2024** – Vitalik registered the ENS domain `dacc.eth`, signalling continued commitment.  
  - **January 2025** – He published a follow‑up article, *“d/acc: One Year Later”*, refining and expanding the framework.

---

## What Does the “d” Stand For?

The letter **“d”** is deliberately multi‑dimensional:

| Meaning | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| **Defensive** | Prioritise technologies that make defence easier than offence (e.g., cybersecurity, biodefence). |
| **Decentralisation** | Prevent concentration of power by distributing technological control. |
| **Democratic** | Ensure open access and broad participation in technology development. |
| **Differential** | Accelerate **selectively** – not all technologies should advance at the same speed; focus on those with clear societal benefits. |

---

## d/acc vs. e/acc – Key Differences

| Aspect | **e/acc (Effective Accelerationism)** | **d/acc (Defensive Accelerationism)** |
|--------|----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Stance on growth** | Unconditional acceleration of all technology | Selective, cautious acceleration |
| **AI attitude** | Let AI evolve freely, even to superintelligence | Emphasise human‑AI collaboration and augmentation; prioritise safety measures |
| **Power structure** | Often tolerant of elite‑driven progress | Explicitly favours decentralisation as a first‑class principle |
| **Risk management** | Risks are acceptable for the sake of progress | Requires detailed contingency plans; a defence‑heavy world is inherently safer |

---

## Three Core Principles of d/acc

1. **Accelerate with decentralisation**  
   Push technological boundaries while actively distributing control to avoid single points of failure.

2. **Defence over offence**  
   Build systems that are more useful for protecting people than for harming them – e.g., strong encryption, open‑source vaccines, and robust information verification.

3. **Democratise technology**  
   Ensure that new tools are accessible, auditable, and usable by ordinary people, not just governments or corporations.

---

## Practical Domains of Application

Vitalik has outlined several concrete areas where d/acc principles can be applied:

- **Biodefence & public health**  
  Verifiable open‑source vaccines, improved air filtration and ventilation, pocket‑sized air quality sensors.

- **Cyber & information defence**  
  Community‑based fact‑checking (e.g., Community Notes), cryptographic tools to combat disinformation, and resilient communication networks.

- **Cryptography & privacy**  
  Zero‑knowledge proofs (ZK‑SNARKs), fully homomorphic encryption, and other privacy‑preserving technologies.

- **Prediction markets**  
  Used as information‑aggregation tools to anticipate and mitigate emerging threats.

- **Decentralised funding**  
  Mechanisms like quadratic funding and retroactive public goods funding to support socially beneficial projects without central gatekeeping.

---

## Relationship with Cryptocurrency

Vitalik explicitly connects d/acc to the crypto ecosystem in three ways:

1. **Shared values** – Decentralisation, censorship resistance, and openness are central to both.  
2. **Community overlap** – The crypto community is a natural early adopter and tester of d/acc‑aligned tools.  
3. **Technical synergy** – Many d/acc tools (prediction markets, ZK‑proofs, DAOs) are built on or benefit from blockchain infrastructure.

---

## Evolution and Current Status

- **Late 2024** – The **“d/aDDy” (d/acc Discovery Day)** was held during the Devcon conference, bringing together experts in biodefence, cyberdefence, information defence, and neurotechnology.  
- **AI safety challenges** – Vitalik proposed two complementary approaches to managing AI risks:  
  - *Liability frameworks* that assign responsibility to developers and users.  
  - A hardware‑based **“pause button”** – global coordination on compute resources to temporarily halt dangerous AI training runs.  
- **Core message** – In a world of accelerating technological change, **decentralised defences reduce the likelihood of catastrophic scenarios**, while preserving openness and individual freedom.
